About Me

I’m a second year UG student at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur majoring in Mechanical Engineering. But my interests are mainly in the areas of Deep Learning and its applications. I have explored the fields of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning and am particularly fascinated by Deep Generative Models, Attention and Transformer based models, and the advancements in Object detection and segmentation using Deep Learning methods. I also had some exposure of Reinforcement Learning while doing the Udacity Deep Reinforcement Learning Nanodegree, I studied Monte-Carlo learning, Temporal learning, Q-learning and related topics and solved some cool projects.

Currently, I am working under Prof. Pabitra Mitra of IIT Kharagpur and Prof. Raktim Bhattacharya of Texas A&M University on the topic of Deep Learning based Structural Health Management Systems. I am also a part of Kharagpur Data Analytics Group (KDAG) which is a society for ML student research- we weekly discuss latest research papers and work colaboratively on projects.
